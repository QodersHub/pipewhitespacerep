About Us { White Space }

Big Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence are foreseen to provide unprecedented step changes in cost and value across the Oil&Gas industry, from Exploration success and Field Development turnaround times to drilling, production and maintenance optimization and improved Safety performance. Use of Analytics is estimated to generate $200B added revenue annually industry wide. As experienced Oil&Gas professionals we know that these promises are within reach, but we also feel that a different approach is needed to accelerate the journey. For example, many AI applications are developed in functional silos, lack transparency and replicability, are software-focussed and ignore integration across the value chain. These solutions typically lead to isolated successes and only small incremental improvements. To fully realize the potential of Analytics for Oil&Gas, we believe essential ingredients are tailored transparency, integrated domain expertise and collaborative analytics. Our value proposition is to offer all three.
